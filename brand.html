Hyyperlink Brand Guidelines

Logo

The Hyyperlink logo represents a key architectural innovation inherent to the Hyyperlink protocol. The geometric design symbolizes the DAG (Directed Acyclic Graph) structure and the interconnected nature of the network.

Typography

Hyyperlink uses the Lekton font family for its logotype and headings. This monospace typeface reflects our technical foundation while maintaining readability.

Name Usage

Consistency in naming is crucial for brand recognition.

Correct Usage:

  • Hyyperlink - For starting sentences and titles
  • hyyperlink - For general usage
  • HYY - Currency ticker

Incorrect Usage:

  • HyyperLink
  • hyyperLink
  • hyper-link
  • hyyper-link

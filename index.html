Roadmap

Milestone 1 - Testnet Launch

Testnet enables us to get reports about what works, what doesn't, what could be better, and what we need to fix before mainnet. We'll be using this milestone to get the network ready for prime time and build the finer details of our vision.

Milestone 2 - Rewards Program

Testnet tokens are great, but you need a way to say thanks to the OG's who help make this thing work. The rewards program tracks who is engaging on socials, using the network, giving feedback, etc. Thanks in advance!

Milestone 3 - Token & NFT Platform

Let's create tokens and NFTs on Hyyperlink! In this milestone we give easy access to creators and devs to launch their NFT or token projects on the network. This phase of testnet will be fun, but also critical to getting it right before mainnet.

Milestone 4 - DeFi Capabilities

This is special sauce behind Hyyperlink! We're building liquidity pools, swaps, AMM, and more as native features of the protocol.

Objective A: Native Liquidity Pools

Built directly into the protocol, providing efficient decentralized trading and automated market making.

Objective B: Native Swaps

Seamless token exchanges directly in the protocol, without relying on third-party services.

Milestone 5 - Public Distribution

Fair and transparent distribution of HYY tokens to the public through a decentralized allocation mechanism.

Milestone 6 - Mainnet Launch

Official launch of the Hyyperlink mainnet with full functionality, including native HYY token and complete protocol features.

FAQ

What makes Hyyperlink different from other cryptocurrencies?

+

Hyyperlink is a highly scalable DeFi-ready L1 network that speeds up confirmation time as transaction volume increases. No blocks, no chains, no extra waiting for confirmations.

What does Hyyperlink actually do?

+

TL;DR - Hyyperlink is a high speed network integrating DeFi at the protocol level.

Hyyperlink removes the need for L2 solutions and third party services to accomplish things like liquidity pools, smart routed swaps, AMM, and other common DeFi tasks. It's all built into the network so everything is in one place, natively supported in the protocol and fast.

How does Hyyperlink compare to Ethereum, Solana, Bitcoin?

+
  • Ethereum transactions take longer to confirm, cost more to send, and programming on top of it requires a little bit more learning.
  • Solana is faster, but is still a linear blockchain that slows down during times of high volume.
  • Bitcoin is slowest, but requires a lot of extra tech on top of it to do more than basic transactions.

Hyyperlink is fast, easy to use, and has a lot of features built in, and because of our unique DAG structure, it's scalable and can deliver fast confirmation times when you need them most.

For a thorough breakdown of what makes Hyyperlink great, check out our Key Information Takeaways.

How fast are transactions?

+

Transactions are very fast, and get faster as more volume is added to the network. Hyyperlink doesn't wait for blocks, block times, or miners; there are no blocks, and our miners don't have anything to do with transactions - transactions are added to the DAG as soon as they are received, with each transaction helping to confirm multiple other transactions at the same time.

How do supply & tokenomics work?

+

Hyyperlink's supply is influenced by two factors:

  • Fee burning: Decreases supply by burning all transaction fees from the global supply.
  • Mining: Increases supply by a target of 2% annually. You can try our Key Discovery Mining system here on our demo page.

What can I do with Hyyperlink?

+

Hyyperlink is an L1 network ready for you to launch your next token, NFT, or DeFi application. The network is engineered from the ground up to be easily understood by developers and users alike. The code is written in popular and easily understood languages like Go, Rust, and Typescript.

How can I get involved?

+

Join the discord and start asking questions :) Right now we're in testnet phase, so as new bits of software and things are released, we'll need people testing them out and giving feedback.

Is Hyyperlink based on or forked from another project?

+

Hyyperlink is not a fork or derivative of another project. It's built from the ground up in the USA. This testnet phase is the opportunity for OG's to see something new and watch it grow from the ground up, first hand.

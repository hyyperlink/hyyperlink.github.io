Roadmap

Milestone 1 - Testnet Launch

Initial release of the Hyyperlink testnet, featuring core wallet functionality and key discovery mining. Early adopters can begin testing the network, providing valuable feedback for mainnet preparation.

Milestone 2 - Token & NFT Platform

Introduction of token creation capabilities and NFT support. Developers can create custom tokens and NFTs on the Hyyperlink network, expanding the ecosystem's functionality.

Milestone 3 - Smart Contracts

Launch of smart contract capabilities with developer tools and SDK. Opening the platform for DApp development and enabling complex programmable transactions.

Milestone 4 - Cross-Network Transfers

Implementation of cross-network functionality enabling seamless asset transfer between major blockchain networks. Enhanced interoperability features for a connected ecosystem.

Milestone 5 - Mainnet Launch

Official launch of the Hyyperlink mainnet, incorporating all tested features and improvements from the testnet phase. The network goes live with full functionality, security measures, and community participation.

FAQ

What makes Hyyperlink different from other cryptocurrencies?

+

Hyyperlink introduces a revolutionary approach to cryptocurrency that eliminates competition between miners. Unlike Bitcoin or Ethereum, where miners compete for rewards, Hyyperlink miners work independently to discover special cryptographic keys. This means anyone can mine without expensive hardware or joining mining pools, making it truly decentralized and energy-efficient.

How does Key Discovery Mining work?

+

Key Discovery Mining is like finding digital treasures. Your computer generates special cryptographic keys, and when it finds one with rare properties (like several zeros at the start), you receive a reward. The rarer the key you find, the bigger the reward. It's simple, fair, and you can mine independently on any computer without competing with others.

What's unique about Hyyperlink's token supply?

+

Hyyperlink starts with zero tokens and grows naturally through mining. The supply is balanced by two forces: new tokens are created through mining, while transaction fees are permanently removed from circulation. This creates a self-regulating economy where network usage directly influences the token supply, helping maintain long-term value.

How fast are transactions?

+

Hyyperlink uses a modern DAG (Directed Acyclic Graph) structure instead of a traditional blockchain, allowing multiple transactions to be processed simultaneously. This means faster confirmations and better scalability as the network grows, without the bottlenecks found in traditional cryptocurrencies.

What can I do with Hyyperlink?

+

Hyyperlink is designed to be a versatile digital currency. You can mine tokens, send and receive payments, and participate in the network's growth. Future updates will bring smart contracts, mobile wallets, and cross-chain capabilities, making Hyyperlink a comprehensive platform for digital transactions and decentralized applications.

How do I start mining Hyyperlink?

+

Getting started with Hyyperlink mining is simple: just download our wallet, create an account, and enable mining. You can mine on any computer - no special hardware required. The wallet includes an easy-to-use mining interface, and you'll start earning rewards as soon as your computer discovers special keys. The more zeros at the start of a discovered key, the bigger your reward!

Is Hyyperlink secure?

+

Yes! Hyyperlink uses advanced cryptography and a reputation-based system to ensure network security. Every transaction is cryptographically signed and verified across the network. Our unique DAG structure and consensus mechanism provide strong protection against common cryptocurrency attacks while maintaining high performance and reliability.

Is Hyyperlink based on or forked from another project?

+

No - Hyyperlink is completely original and built from the ground up. Written in Go, it's not a fork, copy, or derivative of any existing cryptocurrency. Every aspect of Hyyperlink, from its innovative Key Discovery Mining to its DAG architecture, has been carefully designed and implemented from scratch to create a truly unique and forward-thinking digital currency platform.
